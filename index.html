UNIPRO

UNICAP Production Allocation and Ending Inventory Workpaper Tool

Background

UNIPRO supports producer computations under the simplified production method and modified simplified production method without a historic absorption ratio election.

Users paste account-level amounts, confirm what each account represents, and compare the amount of additional Section 263A costs capitalized to ending inventory under each method.

UNIPRO is a computational workpaper. This version is built for the FIFO SPM and MSPM workflow and treats raw materials as one aggregate FIFO pool. It does not determine whether a taxpayer is required or permitted to use a method, whether a method change is required, or whether any election or procedural requirement has been satisfied.

Developed by Jaime Garcia de Paredes and Briauna Williams-Henderson.

Notice and Disclaimer

CONFIDENTIAL AND PROPRIETARY. This calculator is intended only for authorized use.

This tool provides a computational workpaper based solely on user-entered information. It is not tax, legal, accounting, penalty-protection, transaction, or other professional advice.

Results may be incomplete or inaccurate if amounts are incomplete, UNICAP bucket selections are incorrect, book or financial statement amounts are used without tax adjustments, or applicable procedural requirements are not considered.

The user must review all UNICAP bucket selections, assumptions, eligibility, elections, method-change requirements, and computations before use or reliance.

Terminology

Information Needed

Obtain the following before completing the workpaper:

  • Federal tax trial balance with current-year costs and separate beginning and ending inventory balances.
  • Beginning raw materials, resale inventory, work in process, finished goods, and beginning additional Section 263A layer.
  • Current-year raw-material purchases, resale purchases, Section 471 production costs, and additional Section 263A costs.
  • Ending raw materials and the current-year resale and production costs remaining on hand.
  • Detailed support for summarized COGS, purchases, production costs, inventory variances, reserves, write-downs, and book-tax adjustments.
  • The taxpayer's Section 471 capitalization method, selected UNICAP method, mixed service cost method, and Producer de minimis rule determination.
  • All current-year labor and trade or business cost accounts used in the mixed service allocation ratio.

Required Inputs

Enter an amount for each missing item. Enter 0 when the taxpayer has no amount in that category.
